<p><p>The capillary electrophoresis systems market is categorized into automatic and semi-automatic systems. Automatic capillary electrophoresis systems offer a fully automated process, enhancing efficiency and reducing human error by handling sample loading, analysis, and results reporting autonomously. In contrast, semi-automatic systems require some manual input, providing flexibility while still facilitating efficient separations.
