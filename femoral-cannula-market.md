<p><strong>The Femoral Cannula Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unipolar</li><li>Bipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The femoral cannula market consists of two main types: unipolar and bipolar cannulas. Unipolar cannulas are designed for single-direction blood flow and are commonly used in various medical procedures where a streamlined approach is necessary. In contrast, bipolar cannulas facilitate bidirectional flow, allowing for more complex applications such as simultaneous inflow and outflow during surgeries. Both types are essential for effective perfusion during cardiopulmonary bypass and other critical interventions, reflecting the increasing demand for advanced medical devices.</p></p>
