<p><p>The roll-core transformers market is divided into two primary types: dry type and oil-immersed type. Dry type transformers use air for cooling and are ideal for applications requiring low maintenance and environmental safety, making them suitable for indoor installations. In contrast, oil-immersed transformers utilize insulating oil for cooling and are preferred for high-power applications due to their efficiency and ability to handle larger loads. Each type serves different industry needs, emphasizing performance, safety, and application-specific advantages.</p></p>
