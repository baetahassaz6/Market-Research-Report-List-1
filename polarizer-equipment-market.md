<p><strong>The Polarizer Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorptive Polarizers</li><li>Beam-Splitting Polarizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polarizer equipment market features two primary types: absorptive polarizers and beam-splitting polarizers. Absorptive polarizers selectively filter light, absorbing certain polarization states while allowing others to pass through, commonly utilized in photography and displays. Beam-splitting polarizers, on the other hand, divide incoming light into two orthogonal polarization states, often employed in optical devices, lasers, and imaging systems. Both types enhance image quality and control light behavior, catering to various industrial, commercial, and scientific applications.</p></p>
