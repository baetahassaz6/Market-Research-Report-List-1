<p><p>The Montmorillonite clay market primarily consists of two types: sodium bentonite and calcium bentonite. Sodium bentonite is characterized by its high absorbency and swelling properties, making it ideal for applications such as drilling fluids, foundry sands, and environmental barriers. In contrast, calcium bentonite exhibits lower swelling capabilities but offers greater viscosity and is commonly used in cat litter, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals. Both types are valued for their unique chemical and physical properties, serving diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
