<p><strong>The Non-Alcoholic Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Limit Fermentation</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-alcoholic beer market comprises two main types: limit fermentation and dealcoholization methods. Limit fermentation involves controlling the fermentation process to produce beer with low alcohol content, allowing some alcohol to remain while enhancing flavor. In contrast, the dealcoholization method removes alcohol from fully fermented beer, typically using techniques like vacuum distillation or reverse osmosis. Both approaches aim to provide consumers with flavorful beer alternatives that align with health-conscious lifestyles, catering to the growing demand for non-alcoholic beverages.</p></p>
