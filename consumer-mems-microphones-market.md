<p><p>The Consumer MEMS Microphones market is divided into two main types: analog and digital. Analog MEMS microphones output continuous voltage signals, making them simpler and cost-effective, suitable for applications with basic audio needs. In contrast, digital MEMS microphones convert sound into digital signals, allowing for enhanced sound quality and integration with modern digital systems. Digital microphones are often used in advanced devices like smartphones and wearables, where processing and noise cancellation capabilities are essential for improved user experience.</p></p>
