<p><strong>The Automotive Emission Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-dispersive Infrared (NDIR) Analyzers</li><li>Flame Ionization Detector (FID)</li><li>Chemiluminescence Analyzer (CLA)</li><li>Constant Volume Sampler (CVS)</li><li>Other Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive emission analyzer market includes various technologies for exhaust gas measurement. Non-dispersive Infrared (NDIR) analyzers detect gases like CO2 and CO through infrared absorption. Flame Ionization Detectors (FID) measure hydrocarbons by ionizing them in a flame. Chemiluminescence Analyzers (CLA) quantify NOx emissions via light emission from reactions. Constant Volume Samplers (CVS) collect a representative gas sample from a vehicle's exhaust. Other types encompass emerging technologies and hybrid systems aimed at enhancing emission testing accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
