<p><p>The zero backlash coupling market comprises four main types: Metal Disc, Composite Disc, Bellows, and Elastomer Jaw couplings. Metal Disc couplings offer high torque transmission with minimal backlash, ideal for high precision applications. Composite Disc couplings combine lightweight materials and flexibility, reducing weight while maintaining performance. Bellows couplings provide excellent misalignment compensation and torsional stiffness, suitable for high-speed applications. Elastomer Jaw couplings use flexible elastomeric elements, absorbing shock and vibration, making them suitable for various industrial uses.</p></p>
