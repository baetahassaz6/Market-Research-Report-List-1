<p><strong>The Fruit Jellies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl Pectin (HMP)</li><li>Low Methoxyl Pectin (LMP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fruit jellies market is primarily segmented into types based on pectin content: High Methoxyl Pectin (HMP) and Low Methoxyl Pectin (LMP). HMP requires higher sugar concentrations and acidic conditions to gel, making it suitable for traditional fruit preserves. In contrast, LMP can gel with lower sugar levels and works well in low-calorie products and applications with varying fruit types. These characteristics cater to diverse consumer preferences, balancing taste, texture, and health considerations in fruit jelly products.</p></p>
