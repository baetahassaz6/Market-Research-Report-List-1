<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet MRI</li><li>Superconducting Magnet MRI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI machines market is primarily divided into two types: Permanent Magnet MRI and Superconducting Magnet MRI. </p><p>Permanent Magnet MRI uses strong permanent magnets, which are less expensive and more portable, making them suitable for smaller facilities. They typically have lower magnetic field strengths. </p><p>Superconducting Magnet MRI, on the other hand, utilizes high-strength superconducting magnets, providing superior image quality and detailed scans. This type is commonly found in larger hospitals and advanced imaging centers due to its higher cost and space requirements.</p></p>
