<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Module Type Photonic Integrated Circuit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Photonic Integrated Circuit</li><li>Active Photonic Integrated Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photonic integrated circuit (PIC) market is divided into two primary types: passive and active PICs. Passive photonic integrated circuits do not require external power to operate; they manage signal routing, filtering, and splitting through components like waveguides and couplers. In contrast, active photonic integrated circuits incorporate electronic components, using power to manipulate signals, enabling functionalities such as modulation, amplification, and detection. Both types are essential for applications in telecommunications, data centers, and sensor technologies.</p></p>
