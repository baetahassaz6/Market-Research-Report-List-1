<p><strong>The Residential Water Softeners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Salt Based Water Softeners</li><li>Salt Free Water Softeners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential water softeners market primarily features two types: salt-based and salt-free water softeners. Salt-based water softeners use ion exchange technology to remove hardness minerals like calcium and magnesium, effectively preventing scale buildup and improving water quality, often requiring regular salt refills. In contrast, salt-free water softeners utilize alternative technologies, such as template-assisted crystallization, to condition water without eliminating hardness minerals. This option is maintenance-free and eco-friendly, appealing to homeowners looking for sustainable solutions.</p></p>
