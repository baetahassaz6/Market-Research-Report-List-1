<p><p>The Wagyu beef market is categorized into three main types: Fullblood Wagyu, Purebred Wagyu, and Crossbred Wagyu. Fullblood Wagyu comes from purebred lineage with no genetic cross, prized for its high marbling and flavor. Purebred Wagyu contains a significant percentage of Wagyu genetics, often resulting from a mix of Fullblood and other elite breeds. Crossbred Wagyu blends Wagyu with other cattle breeds, producing more affordable beef while still retaining some of the desirable marbling characteristics. Each type serves different consumer preferences and market demands.</p></p>
