<p><p>The Industrial Plastic Granulators market includes various types designed for specific applications. Beside-the-Press Granulators are compact units placed near injection molding machines, ideal for quick recycling. Central Granulators are larger systems, often used in centralized recycling operations, processing bulk materials efficiently. Heavy-Duty Granulators handle tough materials and are built for high-volume production. Thermoforming Granulators are tailored for trimming scrap from thermoforming processes, ensuring optimal size reduction for reuse. Each type enhances efficiency in plastic recycling and manufacturing.</p></p>
