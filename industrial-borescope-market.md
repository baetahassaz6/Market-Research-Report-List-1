<p><ul><li>Flexible Borescopes</li><li>Rigid Borescopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial borescope market includes two main types: flexible borescopes and rigid borescopes. Flexible borescopes consist of a long, pliable tube that allows inspection in hard-to-reach areas by navigating bends and obstacles. Rigid borescopes, on the other hand, are inflexible and usually offer higher image clarity, making them ideal for direct line-of-sight inspections. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, facilitating maintenance and quality control by providing visual access to confined spaces.</p></p>
