<p><p>The full-body CT market comprises two primary types: CT scanners and PET-CT scanners. CT scanners utilize X-rays to generate detailed cross-sectional images of the body, aiding in the diagnosis of various conditions. PET-CT scanners combine positron emission tomography with CT imaging, providing both functional and anatomical insights for more accurate diagnosis, particularly in oncology. These imaging technologies are essential in early disease detection, treatment planning, and monitoring, driving their demand in hospitals and diagnostic centers globally.</p></p>
