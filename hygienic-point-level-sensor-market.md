<p><p>The Hygienic Point Level Sensor Market is categorized into two primary types: contact and non-contact sensors. Contact type sensors directly interact with the medium, ensuring precise level measurement in hygienic applications, commonly used in food and pharmaceutical industries. Non-contact type sensors utilize technologies such as radar or ultrasonic waves to gauge levels without physical contact, which minimizes contamination risk. Both types cater to various hygiene standards, providing reliable solutions for monitoring liquid and solid levels in sensitive environments.</p></p>
